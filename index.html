Salt üßÇ

Avoid Iodized table salt: This type of table salt is highly processed, refined, and bleached. They add anti-caking agent so the salt flows out easily. All the natural minerals are bleached away and the flavor is straight up sodium chloride. You can get iodine from a good diet or supplements. It’s not worth eating crappy salt for it.

Use Redmond real salt(cooking),Celtic sea salt(finishing,Also has cooking salt),Himalayan pink salt,Maldon sea salt

Cereal ü•Ñ

Buy Cereals made from whole grains, not corn, rice, and refined grains. No more than 10 grams of sugar per serving, ideally less and at least 3 grams of fiber & protein per serving - they make you feel full and satisfy your hunger.

Chicken üêî

Buy Organic chicken

Avoid Conventional chicken

Organic chicken means 100% organic non-GMO feed, no animal byproducts, no antibiotics, outdoor access, no irradiation, no pesticides. The only place you can buy pasture raised chicken meat is from a local co-op or farmers market.

Yogurt

Buy100% grass-fed, organic, Greek style yogurt: the best yogurt you can possibly eat is 100% grass-fed, organic, Greek style yogurt. The nutritional profile of organic and grass-fed yogurt is fantastic, and Greek style yogurt is strained which increases the protein and decreases the lactose.

Avoid Yogurt made with milk that is not organic or grass-fed and Yogurt Containing added sugar, starches or natural flavors.Avoid buying flavored yogurts of any kind as they are loaded with sugar and cancel the probiotic effect. If you can’t find 100% grass-fed, buy organic, Do not eat conventional dairy because the cows eat GMO grains.
